My Heritage, My Country, My Culture

About me!

My name is Sandra Chinouya. I was born in the beautyful country of Zimbabwe on the Estern side of the country
This side of the country is called the Estern Highlands or Manicaland, that is dominated by the Samanyika tribe.
I wasnt only born in this region but it was the home of my great grandparents and where my father grew up as well.
This is the place l call home and everything l know about my culture started in this area that is rich in every way.
Manicaland might be small but it is very rich in minerals and other natural resaurces, its a piece that fits right
making Zimbabwe the beautyful country it is and I am proud to be part of this beautyful culture and legacy.
